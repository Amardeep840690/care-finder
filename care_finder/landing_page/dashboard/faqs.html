Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by visiting our Appointment section, selecting your preferred hospital, department, doctor, and time slot, then confirming your booking.
Do I need to register before booking?
Registration is optional for first-time bookings, but creating an account helps you track, reschedule, or cancel appointments easily.
Is emergency booking available?
Yes, our Emergency Appointment option allows quick booking for urgent cases and assigns the first available doctor immediately.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule through your account dashboard or by contacting the hospital directly.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept online payments via credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking, as well as cash payments at the hospital.
