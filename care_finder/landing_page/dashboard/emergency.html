🚑 First Aid Essentials

  • CPR: Push hard & fast in the center of the chest, 100–120 compressions/min.
  • Stop bleeding: Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth.
  • Burns: Cool under running water for 20 minutes, cover with sterile cloth.
  • Choking: Perform Heimlich maneuver — abdominal thrusts until breathing resumes.
